Brunch El Nido is a cozy café and brunch restaurant located at Unit-H Rizal Street, El Nido, Palawan, Philippines. It is known for its international brunch menu featuring comfort classics and creative breakfast options in an inviting, air-conditioned space popular with both travelers and locals. 

Atmosphere and Concept
The café offers a relaxed, family-friendly environment with board games, soft lighting, and upstairs lounge seating. It’s a cool retreat from the tropical heat and doubles as a casual coworking spot thanks to reliable Wi-Fi and attentive service. Guests frequently note the friendly staff and laid-back vibe ideal for lingering over coffee or brunch. 

Menu Highlights
Brunch El Nido serves international breakfast and lunch dishes such as eggs Benedict, lox bagels, Nutella waffles, and paninis. The California Breakfast bagel and Filipino breakfast are customer favorites. Drinks range from Vietnamese and caramel lattes to fruit smoothies and mimosas, with vegetarian and vegan options available. 

Reception and Recognition
The restaurant holds a 4.7/5 Google rating and ranks among the top eateries in El Nido on Tripadvisor. Reviewers praise the flavorful dishes, generous portions, cleanliness, and staff hospitality. Occasional wait-time complaints appear, but overall sentiment highlights consistent quality and an enjoyable brunch experience. 

Why It Matters
Brunch El Nido has become a staple stop for visitors exploring Palawan’s beach town, offering a Western-style café experience with Filipino warmth. Its combination of creative brunch fare, comfortable setting, and consistent service makes it one of the most recommended breakfast spots in El Nido.
