Our Story

A Cool Escape in
the Heart of Paradise

Nestled on Rizal Street in El Nido, Palawan, Brunch El Nido is your island refuge — air-conditioned, beautifully mural-adorned, and brimming with the warm energy of Filipino hospitality. Whether you're fueling up before an island-hopping adventure or lingering over mimosas with friends, you're exactly where you're supposed to be.
